Welcome to Marrakesh

Marrakech is a city of moments. Gaze at the iconic Koutoubia as the call to prayer rings out at sunset, wander through the Bab Doukkala market and sample the fragrant spices, or pull up a seat at a communal table and dine in the bustling night market. The focal point of the city’s rambling morphology is the Djemaa el-Fna, its finest sights the sculpted Bahia Palace, the Medersa Ali Ben Youssef and the green cacti garden of the Jardin Majorelle.
